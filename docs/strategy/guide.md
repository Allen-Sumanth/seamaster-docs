# Strategy Guide

## Bot Archetypes

### The Forager
*   **Abilities**: `Harvest`, `SpeedBoost`
*   **Role**: Rapidly collects algae and returns to bank.
*   **Weakness**: Fragile. Dies to `SelfDestruct`.

### The Scout
*   **Abilities**: `Scout`, `SpeedBoost`
*   **Role**: Maps the board, identifying **Poisonous Algae**.
*   **Tip**: Spawn one early to update your global map.

### The Saboteur (Kamikaze)
*   **Abilities**: `SelfDestruct`, `SpeedBoost` (sometimes `Shield`)
*   **Role**: Runs into enemy clusters and explodes.
*   **Cost Efficiency**: Trading 15 scraps to kill 3 enemy bots (30 scraps) is a winning move.

### The Guardian
*   **Abilities**: `Shield`, `SelfDestruct`
*   **Role**: Parks near your Bank to deter Lockpickers.

## Synergies
*   **Shield + SelfDestruct**: Allows you to survive your own explosion (requires careful implementation).
*   **Scout + Harvest**: A heavy bot that harvests safely but moves slowly.
